Privacy Policy – MJH Battle Royale Frenzy

Last updated: 7 May 2026

This privacy policy explains how “MJH Battle Royale Frenzy” (the game) handles information when you play in your browser or install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

Data Collected

This game does not collect any personal data such as your name, email, IP address, device ID, or location. All gameplay is processed locally in your browser.

Local Storage Usage

To improve your experience, the game uses your device’s local storage (browser storage) only to:

  • Save your all‑time high score on your device.

This data is never sent to any server or shared with third parties.

Ads and Third‑Party Services

Currently this version of the game does not contain any advertisements or third‑party tracking scripts (such as analytics or advertisement SDKs). If these features are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated.

Children’s Privacy

This game is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from visitors under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last updated” date will be revised.
